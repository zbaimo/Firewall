====================================================
  How to Add Favicon (Optional)
====================================================

The 404 error for favicon.ico is cosmetic only.
To fix it:

Method 1: Use Online Generator
--------------------------------

1. Visit: https://favicon.io/
2. Create your favicon (text, image, or emoji)
3. Download the generated files
4. Place favicon.ico in: web/static/favicon.ico

Method 2: Use Existing Image
--------------------------------

If you have a logo:

1. Convert to .ico format (use online converter)
2. Save as: web/static/favicon.ico
3. Restart container

--------------------------------
